# Company Choice — Milestone 0

## Company Selection

I chose Nexova Solutions because their core business problems are the most financially urgent of the four companies. Their talent selection operation is their primary revenue source and runs entirely on manual effort, with 40 consultants reading up to 80 CVs per search. Their customer support team is breaching the 24-hour SLA they promised clients, delivering 48 hours instead. These are direct threats to revenue and client retention.

## Department Interests

**Talent Selection Operations** is Nexova's primary revenue source. Automating CV scoring means consultants close more placements without adding headcount.

**Customer Support Outsourcing** is the highest financial risk. Thirty agents work with no knowledge base and miss their SLA every day. Automation stops active revenue loss.

## Technical Milestone

I am most looking forward to building the RAG system over the candidate database. A consultant types a plain language query and gets ranked candidate results in seconds instead of reading CVs manually.

## My AI Agent Idea

The agent I want to build searches and ranks candidates for the Talent Selection team using plain language queries. It needs candidate CVs, skills, languages, and experience records. It connects to the applicant tracking system and delivers ranked results directly into the consultant workflow.
